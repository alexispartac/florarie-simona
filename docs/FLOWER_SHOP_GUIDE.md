## üåº Flower Care Instructions

### General Care Tips
1. **Water Daily** - Keep vase filled with fresh water
2. **Trim Stems** - Cut 1-2 cm at a 45¬∞ angle every 2-3 days
3. **Remove Dead Leaves** - Keep only healthy foliage
4. **Avoid Direct Sunlight** - Place in indirect light
5. **Keep Cool** - Away from heat sources

### Specific Flower Care

#### Roses
- Water: Daily
- Temperature: 15-20¬∞C
- Lifespan: 7-10 days
- Tip: Remove thorns below water line

#### Tulips
- Water: Keep water level low (5cm)
- Temperature: Cool room
- Lifespan: 5-7 days
- Tip: They continue to grow in vase

#### Sunflowers
- Water: Change water every 2 days
- Temperature: Room temperature
- Lifespan: 7-12 days
- Tip: Remove lower leaves

#### Lilies
- Water: Keep water fresh
- Temperature: Cool room
- Lifespan: 7-10 days
- Tip: Remove pollen to prevent staining
